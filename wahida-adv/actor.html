Odette, the lovely and beautiful swan

Odette is the daughter of a baker and the sister of a caring sporty rider. She takes care of the village children, elders and especially her small family. Odette is very modest. This quality stands out the most when the Fairy Queen tells her she is the chosen one.

Daniel, the handsome prince

Daniel's mother wants to see the future queen soon and wonders when her son will finally get married. He, in turn, wants to wait for the right one. He is firmly convinced that a special or higher status says nothing about one's personality.

Fairy queen, cary and love in one

The fairy queen of the forest is liked by everyone for her caring and love. She would protect the forest and its inhabitants with her life.

Rothbart, the egotistical and power-hungry

Rothbart is very power-hungry and wants to have the whole forest in his hands. He wants to turn every living thing in it into an animal. When he hears that Odette is immune to his magicians, he wants to defeat her.

Odile, the spoiled one

There is nothing to say about Odile except that she is very spoiled by her father rothbart. She is not grateful for what she has and wants to be.
