Barbie the swan

Odette, a baker's daughter, follows a unicorn into an enchanted forest where the fairy queen and her cousin, the evil wizard Rothbart, are fighting for dominance. Rothbart transforms Odette into a swan at their first encounter. The fairy queen softens the curse so that Odette regains her human form in the night. The inhabitants of the forest, who are also enchanted, believe that Odette is the one who, according to a prophecy, will succeed in freeing their world from the evil spell with the help of a magic crystal. Together with the unicorn Lila and the troll Erasmus, Odette uncovers the secret of the crystal. When Daniel, the prince of her homeland, gets lost in the magical forest, he falls in love with Odette. Rothbart's spell can only be broken if the prince swears his eternal love to the bearer of the crystal. With a ruse, Rothbart manages to get Prince Daniel to swear this oath to his daughter Odile because he thinks she is Odette. The false oath also breaks the spell over Odette, who regains her human form and flees with the fairy queen and her entourage into the enchanted forest. There a battle ensues between Rothbart, Odette and Prince Daniel, who rushes to help, and the sorcerer is defeated by the power of the crystal.
